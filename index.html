About Anaenge Charity
Foundation

Anaenge Charity Foundation, founded by Caroline Ndikon, is a nonprofit organization located at 6612 Ian Street, New Carroleon, Maryland. As a tax-exempt 501(c)(3), we are dedicated to empowering young women (ages 16â€“21) and unemployed youth by providing skill development programs that foster independence and growth. Our initiatives include hands-on culinary training, equipping participants with practical expertise and opportunities to serve their community.

In addition to skill-building, we operate a food bank to address hunger and provide vital support to individuals and families in need. By combining education, mentorship, and community action, Anaenge Charity Foundation is committed to creating lasting change, helping those we serve build brighter and more sustainable futures.
